NANDAMURI TARAKA RAMA RAO

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr., widely known as Jr NTR, is a prominent Indian actor, producer, and television presenter, primarily recognized for his work in Telugu cinema. Born on May 20, 1983, in Hyderabad, he hails from a distinguished lineage; his grandfather, N. T. Rama Rao Sr., was a legendary Telugu actor and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh.Jr NTR’s cinematic journey commenced as a child artist in films like “Brahmarshi Viswamitra” (1991) and “Ramayanam” (1997), the latter earning the National Film Award for Best Children’s Film. He debuted as a lead actor with “Ninnu Choodalani” in 2001. Subsequent successes, such as “Student No. 1” (2001) and “Aadi” (2002), solidified his status in the industry. Over the years, he has delivered notable performances in films like “Simhadri” (2003), “Yamadonga” (2007), “Brindavanam” (2010), “Temper” (2015), “Nannaku Prematho” (2016), “Janatha Garage” (2016), “Jai Lava Kusa” (2017), and the internationally acclaimed “RRR” (2022), and devara part1 (2024). Beyond acting, Jr NTR has showcased his versatility by hosting television shows, including the inaugural season of “Bigg Boss” in Telugu (2017) and the fifth season of “Evaru Meelo Koteeswarulu” (2021). In his personal life, Jr NTR is married to Lakshmi Pranathi, and the couple is blessed with two sons.
